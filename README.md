# Revenue-Forecasting-YouTube-TV-OTT-
This project focuses on Quarterly Revenue Forecasting across three media platforms.
YouTube (Digital Ad Revenue), TV (Traditional Broadcast Revenue) and OTT (Streaming Platform Revenue)

# The dataset spans Q1 2023 – Q3 2026, including:
Platform-wise revenue (THB Mn)
Total revenue
Platform revenue share (%)
Quarterly growth rate
Average growth
Forecasted next quarter revenue

# Analysis Value:-
# A)Growth Acceleration Phase (2023–2024)-
High quarterly growth (20%, 12%, 11%)
OTT share jumped from 13.33% → 29%+
Strong digital transformation phase
# B)Stabilization Phase (2025–2026)
Growth normalized to ~4–6%
Forecast values stabilized around ~48,000 THB Mn (scaled model output)
Market entering maturity phase

# C)Platform Share Shift
Year	 YouTube   Share 	TV Share	OTT Share
2023 	 ~20%	    ~66%	   ~13%
2026	 ~32%	    ~29%	   ~39%

-OTT becomes the largest revenue contributor by 2026
-TV share continuously declines
-YouTube shows steady upward dominance

# Key Insights:-
-OTT is the fastest-growing revenue stream.
-TV revenue is stable but losing market share.
-Digital platforms (YouTube + OTT) now contribute 70%+ of total revenue.
-Growth rate declining gradually → market saturation beginning.
-Forecast suggests steady but moderate expansion (~4% quarterly growth).
